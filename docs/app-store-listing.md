## Full App Store Description

Nursing school is hard enough without scrambling between five different apps, three textbooks, and a stack of handwritten flashcards that keep falling behind the couch.

Pioneer Nurse puts everything TWU Dallas BSN students need into one free study companion -- built specifically around the courses, exams, and clinical expectations you actually face each semester.

WHAT YOU GET:

AI Study Tutor
Ask anything about patho, pharm, med-surg, or fundamentals and get clear, structured explanations tailored to nursing students. The AI tutor breaks down complex topics into language that clicks, walks you through clinical scenarios step by step, and helps you think through NCLEX-style questions. Every AI response includes a reminder to verify with your instructor and course materials.

Drug Cards with Hold Parameters
Searchable drug reference cards covering the medications you encounter in clinical. Each card includes classification, mechanism of action, nursing considerations, hold parameters, and common side effects -- organized the way your instructors expect you to know them.

Practice Questions with Adaptive Difficulty
NCLEX-style practice questions that adjust to your performance. Get harder questions as you improve and focused review on topics where you need more work. Detailed rationales explain both why the correct answer is right and why the distractors are wrong.

Spaced Repetition Flashcards
Create your own flashcards or study from shared decks aligned to TWU courses. The app schedules reviews using spaced repetition so you see cards right before you would forget them -- the most efficient way to move information into long-term memory.

Clinical Preparation Tools
Organize clinical prep with structured templates for patient assessments, care plans, and pre-clinical research. Walk into clinical feeling prepared instead of panicked.

Smart Study Planner
The app tracks your upcoming exams, weak areas, and study streaks to generate a personalized daily study plan. It tells you what to study, for how long, and why -- so you spend less time deciding and more time learning.

COMPLETELY FREE. No subscriptions. No paywalls. No ads. No "premium tier" that locks the useful features. Everything is available to every student from day one.

PERFECT FOR:

- First-semester students building foundational knowledge
- Second-semester students tackling med-surg and pharmacology
- Third-semester students preparing for complex clinical rotations
- Fourth-semester students doing final NCLEX preparation
- Any TWU BSN student who wants a smarter way to study

WHY THIS EXISTS:

Pioneer Nurse was built by a TWU Dallas nursing student who got tired of piecing together study tools that were not designed for nursing school. Every feature exists because a real student needed it. There is no venture capital, no investor pressure to monetize your data, and no reason this app exists other than to help TWU nursing students succeed.

Your study data stays private. Your AI conversations are not used to train models. Your progress belongs to you.

Welcome to Pioneer Nurse. Study smarter. Pass with confidence.
